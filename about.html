Professional Biography

Ge Lyu (also known as GL) is an experienced software engineer and manager with expertise in artificial intelligence, big data infrastructure, and full-stack development. He currently leads an IT consulting company based in the San Francisco Bay Area, providing strategic engineering solutions to a range of clients.

Originally from Tianjin, China, Ge holds a Bachelor's degree in Ocean Engineering from Tianjin University. He moved to the United States in 2010, later earning a Master of Science in Environmental Management from Samford University in Alabama (2021), followed by a Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Santa Clara University (2024).

Ge worked at Twitter from 2016 until 2023, where he contributed to the development of scalable systems and infrastructure. Following the company's acquisition, he joined a startup, gaining hands-on experience in early-stage innovation and growth.

Today, Ge combines technical depth with leadership insight to support clients across diverse industries. Outside of his professional work, he continues to explore the natural world with a camera in hand and a strong sense of purpose.
